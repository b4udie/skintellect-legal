Skintellect

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 7 July 2026 · Last updated: 7 July 2026

Skintellect is a skincare-ingredient scanner. This policy explains what data the app processes, what stays on your device, what is sent to our servers, and the choices you have. We built the app to keep as much as possible on your device.

The short version.
  • Your photos never leave your device. The app reads the ingredient text on-device and only sends that text for analysis.
  • There is no account. We don't ask for your name, email, or phone number.
  • We use privacy-respecting usage analytics and process purchases through the App Store.
  • We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

1. Who we are

Skintellect ("the app", "we", "us") is developed by Valentyn Bratkevych, an independent developer. For any privacy question or request, contact us at bratkevychv@proton.me. We are the controller of the limited data described below.

2. Data that stays on your device

The following is created and stored only on your device and is never uploaded to us:

  • Photos you capture of product labels. They are used to read the ingredient list on-device and are stored locally (as a small thumbnail) so your scan history can show them.
  • On-device text recognition (OCR). Reading the label happens locally using Apple's Vision framework. The photo itself is not transmitted.
  • Your scan and comparison history, kept in the app's local database.
  • Your free-scan counter, stored in the device Keychain.
  • Your theme preference.

Because this data lives on your device, deleting the app removes it (the free-scan counter in the Keychain is the intentional exception, so the free allowance is not reset by reinstalling).

3. Data sent to our servers

To analyze a product, the app sends our backend only:

  • the recognized ingredient text (the words read from the label — not the photo);
  • the parsed list of ingredients;
  • your app locale (e.g. en, fr) so results are returned in your language;
  • the app version, for diagnostics and compatibility.

These requests are cryptographically signed and sent over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection. They do not include your name, email, device identifiers, or the photo. Our servers are hosted in the European Union.

Service providers used for analysis

To produce the ingredient assessment, our backend passes the ingredient text to trusted processors:

  • Anthropic (Claude) and OpenAI — to interpret and summarize ingredient information. Per their API terms, this input is used to serve the request and is not used to train their models.
  • PubMed (U.S. National Library of Medicine) — to look up public scientific references shown as sources.

Only the ingredient text is shared with these providers — nothing that identifies you.

4. Usage analytics

We use Amplitude to understand how the app is used (for example, which screens are opened and whether a scan completes) so we can improve it. This includes:

  • anonymous usage events and their properties;
  • a randomly generated device/installation identifier and standard technical metadata (app version, device model, OS version) provided by the analytics SDK.

Analytics does not contain your name or email (we don't have them), and we do not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking. Data is processed by Amplitude, Inc. as our processor.

5. Subscriptions and purchases

Skintellect offers optional paid subscriptions. Purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store — we never see or store your payment details. We use RevenueCat to manage entitlement (whether a subscription is active). RevenueCat receives an anonymous app-user identifier and the App Store transaction/receipt needed to validate the subscription. See Apple's Privacy Policy and RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

6. No account, no profiling

The app has no sign-up or login. We do not build advertising profiles, we do not sell or rent personal data, and we do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

7. Data retention

On-device data stays until you delete it or uninstall the app. Ingredient-analysis requests are processed to return your result and may be retained briefly in server logs for security and reliability, then discarded. Analytics data is retained by Amplitude according to our configured retention settings.

8. Children

Skintellect is not directed to children and is intended for users aged 16 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used the app, contact us and we will help.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example under the EU/UK GDPR or the California CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to object to it. Because most data is on your device, you can exercise many of these rights directly by deleting the app. For anything held by us or our processors, email bratkevychv@proton.me and we will respond.

10. International transfers

Our servers are in the EU. Our processors (Anthropic, OpenAI, Amplitude, RevenueCat, Apple) may process data in the United States or other countries under appropriate safeguards such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.

12. Contact

Questions or requests: bratkevychv@proton.me.
