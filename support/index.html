What to include

To help us resolve your issue faster, please mention:

  • a short description of the problem or question;
  • your app version (Settings → About) and your device model & iOS version;
  • for a scan issue, which product you were scanning and what you expected to see.

Common questions

My photos — are they uploaded? No. The app reads the ingredient text on your device and only sends that text for analysis. Photos never leave your device.

Subscriptions & refunds. Purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. To manage or cancel a subscription, open the App Store app → your account → Subscriptions. Refunds are requested through Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.
